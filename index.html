Indian Cuisine

We welcome our diners with a symphony of spices. This intimate restaurant specializes in North Indian cuisine, offering a menu that spans from succulent kebabs to aromatic biryanis. Each dish is a celebration of flavor, crafted to transport you to the bustling streets of Delhi.

MENU

Vegetable Samosas

The filling includes potatoes, peas, carrots, and spices like turmeric, garam masala, and mustard seeds

Vegetable Pakora

ngredients include potatoes, onions, spinach, and cauliflower, seasoned with spices like garam masala, chili powder, and turmeric.

Lentil Fritters (Masala Vada)

Vada, are a savory Indian snack made from lentils and a blend of aromatic spices

Gobi 65 (Cauliflower 65)

South Indian dish Chicken 65 and is prepared by coating cauliflower pieces in a spiced batter made with flour, yogurt, and various seasonings like garlic, ginger, and curry leaves.

Rogan Josh

A staple of Kashmiri cuisine. It consists of braised lamb chunks cooked with gravy made from browned onions, yogurt, garlic, ginger, and aromatic spices

Dal Tadka

It consists of black beans or red kidney beans and whole black lentils, called ‘urad’. The dish is prepared with plenty of ghee and seasonings such as ginger, garlic paste, and chili, and it is slowly cooked in a rich, tomato-based sauce.

Butter Chicken

A dish of tender chicken in a mildly spiced tomato sauce. It’s traditionally cooked in a tandoor (a cylindrical clay oven). The gravy is always made first by boiling down fresh tomato, garlic, and cardamom into a bright red pulp

Malabar fish curry

The curry is typically made with seer, sea bass, tilapia, or sardines. Other ingredients include onions, tomatoes, hot chili peppers, chili powder, curry leaves, coriander, turmeric, garlic, fenugreek, tamarind, oil, and coconut milk

Mughlai Chicken

It consists of chicken pieces cooked in a gravy made with yogurt, onions, nuts, and a variety of spices such as turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, and black pepper.

Chicken Seekh Kabab

juicy interior and slightly crisp exterior, with flavors enhanced by aromatics such as onions, ginger, garlic, green chilies, coriander leaves, and mint leaves.

Lehsuni Nimbu Murgh (Garlic Lemon Chicken)

is a flavorful Indian dish that combines the tanginess of lemon with the pungency of garlic.

Chicken Biryani

Marinated chicken and basmati rice with a rich blend of spices, including saffron, cardamom, and cinnamon. The ingredients are slow-cooked in a sealed pot, allowing the flavors to meld beautifully.

Chana Masala

flavorful North Indian dish made with chickpeas (chana) simmered in a spiced tomato and onion gravy. It’s a vegan and gluten-free curry that pairs well with rice, roti, or naan. The spices typically include garam masala, coriander, and cumin, giving it a warm and hearty flavor profile

Aloo Beans

simple yet flavorful Indian dish consisting of potatoes (aloo) and green beans. It’s a dry curry where the vegetables are stir-fried with spices like cumin seeds, turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala.

Whole Masoor dal

Whole Masoor Dal is rich in protein and fiber, and when cooked, it has a medium consistency that pairs well with rice or flatbreads

Mushroom Masala

is a savory Indian curry that features mushrooms cooked in a rich and aromatic sauce. The sauce typically includes a base of onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, and a variety of spices such as garam masala, turmeric, and cumin.

Indian Rice Pudding (Kheer)

It’s made by simmering rice with milk and sugar, and it’s often flavored with cardamom, raisins, saffron, and nuts like pistachios and almonds.

Indian Milk Balls

a classic Indian dessert. They are small, round, deep-fried balls made from milk solids (khoya) or milk powder, flour, and a leavening agent.

Shrikhand

traditional Indian dessert made from strained yogurt, known for its creamy texture and sweet flavor. It’s often flavored with cardamom, saffron, and nuts

Mango Halwa

A sweet and aromatic Indian dessert. It’s a pudding-like dish made with mango puree, semolina (sooji), sugar, ghee, and flavored with cardamom.
